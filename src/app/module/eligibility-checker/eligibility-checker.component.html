🧮 Key Loan Repayment Metrics

We use the formula:

EMI = [ P × r × (1 + r)n ] / [ (1 + r)n − 1 ]
  • P = Loan Amount
  • r = Monthly Interest Rate (Annual Rate ÷ 12 ÷ 100)
  • n = Tenure in Months

🔹 Example:

Loan Amount: ₹5,00,000
Interest Rate: 10% annual
Tenure: 5 years = 60 months
Monthly Rate = 10 ÷ 12 ÷ 100 = 0.00833

💰 Income Capacity Insights

We deduct monthly expenses from your salary to find your net available income:

Adjusted Income = Monthly Salary − Expenses

🔸 Example:

Monthly Salary: ₹60,000
Expenses: ₹15,000
Adjusted Income = ₹60,000 − ₹15,000 = ₹45,000

📌 Affordability Threshold Check

We check if EMI is less than or equal to 50% of your adjusted income:

Ratio = ( EMI / Adjusted Income ) × 100

If Ratio ≤ 50%, you're eligible.

🔹 Example:

EMI: ₹20,000
Adjusted Income: ₹45,000
Ratio = ( ₹20,000 / ₹45,000 ) × 100 = 44.4%
➤ You are eligible 🎉
